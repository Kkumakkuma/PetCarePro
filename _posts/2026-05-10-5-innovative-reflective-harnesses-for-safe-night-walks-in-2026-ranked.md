## How Does a Reflective Harness Work?

Reflective harnesses utilize high-visibility materials that bounce light back to its source. They typically include reflective stitching, patches, or straps that reflect car headlights and street lights, allowing drivers and pedestrians to see your dog from a distance.

The most effective harnesses employ materials rated under the American National Standards Institute (ANSI) standards for reflectivity, making them highly visible even at night. This innovative technology helps keep your dog safe while giving you peace of mind during late walks.

### Key Features of Reflective Harnesses

1. **Reflective Material:** Quality harnesses like the Ruffwear Front Range use materials designed to reflect a significant amount of light, improving visibility.
2. **Comfort Fit:** A good design ensures it fits well, allowing dogs to move freely without restriction.
3. **Durability:** Look for harnesses with strong stiching and materials that can withstand various outdoor conditions.
