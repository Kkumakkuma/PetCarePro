## How Does a Smart Dog Feeder Work?
A smart dog feeder operates using mechanisms that allow for precise portion control and automated feeding schedules based on your dog’s needs. Using Wi-Fi connectivity, these feeders allow for remote programming via an app on your smartphone. 

Features typically include:
- **Scheduled Feedings:** Set specific times for meals.
- **Portion Control:** Dispense precise amounts based on your pet’s dietary requirements.
- **Video Monitoring:** Some models even let you watch your pet while feeding.
