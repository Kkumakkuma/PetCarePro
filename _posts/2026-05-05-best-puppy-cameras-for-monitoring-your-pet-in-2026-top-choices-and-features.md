## Why You Need a Puppy Camera

### Monitoring Separation Anxiety

Puppyhood is often marked by bouts of separation anxiety. According to a study from the American Kennel Club, about 20% of puppies show signs of stress when left alone. Having a quality camera can allow you to check in on your puppy and soothe your worries.

### Training Aid

Whether you’re teaching basic commands or working on house training, a puppy camera can serve as a valuable tool. I found myself using it to observe how my puppy reacted when I left the room, leading me to adjust my training strategies effectively.

### Safety and Security

Cameras are not just for monitoring behavior; they also serve as a measure of safety. I once caught my dog attempting to chew a non-pet-safe item, which was a situation I could have easily prevented had I been monitoring closely.

## What Most Guides Get Wrong

### Most Cameras Are Not User-Friendly

Many reviews rave about features without addressing how easy or difficult a camera is to set up. I learned the hard way with one brand that took over an hour to get online. **Why this matters**: Time spent wrestling with tech can cause more frustration than benefit.

### Price Doesn’t Equal Quality

Some might assume that higher prices mean better features. For example, the Wyze Cam V3 is nearly half the cost of other models but performs admirably in most settings. **Why this matters**: You might be compromising on a device that provides solid value but costs significantly less.

### Reliability Over Features

Some guides list the latest features but miss common issues like connectivity drops. I had to return a camera that kept losing Wi-Fi connection despite having great specs. **Why this matters**: A reliable camera ensures you can monitor your pet at all times, as I learned the hard way after missing crucial moments.

## Is It Worth It?

So, is investing in a puppy camera worth it? If you find yourself frequently away from home, want to make sure your puppy is safe, or are looking for additional training aids, then, yes, a puppy camera is worth the purchase. However, if you are someone who spends most of your day at home, you may not find it as useful.

### Buyer Profile

- **Worth it if you**: Work long hours, have a puppy that displays signs of anxiety, or frequently leave your pet alone.
- **Skip if you**: Are home the majority of the time and want to avoid an extra gadget.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much should I spend on a puppy camera?

While you can find decent options starting around $35, it's advisable to invest $100 to $200 for reliable features. As mentioned earlier, brands like Petcube and Furbo have higher costs but offer diverse functionalities.

### When should I start using a puppy camera?

You can start using a puppy camera as soon as you bring your puppy home, especially if you'll be away for extended periods. This allows you to monitor their adaptation and any possible anxiety.

### How long will it take to see results with my puppy's behavior?

This varies, of course, based on your puppy's individual traits and the training methods you utilize. However, many owners, including myself, notice improvement within a few weeks of consistent monitoring and training. 

### Can I view my puppy camera on a smartphone?

Absolutely! Most modern puppy cameras, like the ones I've reviewed, offer mobile apps for both iOS and Android, allowing easy access from anywhere.

### Are there subscription fees involved?

Some cameras do come with optional subscription services for advanced features like cloud storage and video history. Brands like Furbo and Blink offer these options, but others like Wyze do not require any subscription at all.
