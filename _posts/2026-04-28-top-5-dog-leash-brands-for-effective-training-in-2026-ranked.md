### Price Doesn't Always Equal Quality
Some leashes are marketed as "premium" and come with a hefty price tag. I tried a leash that cost $70 and ultimately found the $30 strongdog leash performed better for Bruno. **Why this matters:** Don’t fall for branding; instead, focus on how well your chosen leash meets your specific needs, considering what I learned firsthand about various brands.

### Colors and Patterns Confuse Buyers
Several dog owners make the mistake of choosing leashes based solely on aesthetics, overlooking durability and functionality. I once chose a pretty leash that ended up fraying within weeks. **Why this matters:** Function should always trump fashion when it comes to training leashes—what I discovered ultimately made all the difference in my training process.
