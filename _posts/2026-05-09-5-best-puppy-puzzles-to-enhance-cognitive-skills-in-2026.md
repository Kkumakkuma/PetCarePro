## How Can Puppy Puzzles Enhance Cognitive Skills?
Puppy puzzles are specifically designed toys that challenge your dog's mental abilities, encouraging critical thinking and problem-solving skills. By using treats hidden within puzzles, they not only engage your pup’s brain but also help reduce boredom and anxiety. 

- **Cognitive Development:** Just like kids, puppies learn best through play and problem-solving. Engaging them in this manner helps refine their cognitive skills.
- **Boredom Buster:** A bored puppy is a recipe for trouble. Puzzles can prevent destructive behaviors while providing mental stimulation.
- **Bonding Time:** Working together with your puppy on puzzles strengthens your bond and understanding of each other.

### 2. Price Equals Quality
A common misconception is that higher price always means higher quality.

**Why this matters:** I once purchased a $40 puzzle that quickly fell apart, while a $20 option from TRIXIE lasted much longer and was more engaging. Look for user reviews and real-life durability experiences in your research.

### 3. Duration of Interest

**Why this matters:** In my experience, puppies will master puzzles and lose interest. Rotating their toys regularly keeps excitement levels high.
