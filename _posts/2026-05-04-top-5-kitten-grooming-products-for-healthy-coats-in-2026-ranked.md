## What Most Guides Get Wrong

### Grooming Should Be A Hassle-Free Experience

Many guides suggest making grooming an ordeal by pushing for long sessions. 

**Why this matters:** Cats, particularly kittens, can easily become stressed during extended grooming sessions. From my personal experience, short and frequent sessions are better. I found that a 10-minute grooming session every other day would yield better results and keep my kitten calmer.

### Natural Ingredients Are Always Better

While natural products are often praised, some aren’t as effective compared to traditional options.

**Why this matters:** For example, while Earthbath's shampoo worked wonders, another natural product I tried was ineffective in combating matting, forcing me to bathe my kitten repeatedly. This wasted time and effort, and only by trial and error did I realize natural doesn't always mean better.

### Waterless Solutions Are Just As Effective

Many guides undermine the capacity of waterless grooming products.

**Why this matters:** When I used Vet’s Best Waterless Bath, I was surprised by how well it cleaned up my kitten’s mess. I had brush and wash sessions planned for each week, but this quick alternative saved me weeks of extra grooming time.
