## What Factors Determine the Cost of Premium Dog Crates in 2026?
### Material Quality
When it comes to dog crates, the material makes a huge difference in cost. Premium dog crates are often made of either:

- **Heavy-Duty Metal:** These crates can endure serious wear and tear, especially for strong, determined dogs. Brands like **MidWest Homes for Pets** offer metal crates starting around $250, which are not only sturdy but also designed to keep escape artists contained.
- **High-Grade Plastic or Composite:** While not as durable as metal, premium plastic crates like **Petmate Sky Kennel** (approx. $200-$300) can provide good portability and airport travel compliance.

### Size and Customization
The size of the crate directly affects the price. Standard and large crates are usually more expensive:

- **Standard Sizes:** Generally range from $120 to $350 depending on brand and features.
- **Large or Extra-Large Sizes:** Often seen priced from $300 to $700. For instance, **Gunner Kennels** sells heavy-duty crates that start at $600 and go up for larger sizes due to their superior construction designed for safety.

### Brand Reputation
Certain brands simply command a higher price due to their established reliability and warranties:

## How Can I Choose the Right Dog Crate for My Pet?
### Assess Your Dog's Behavior
Understanding your dog's specific behaviors are crucial. Does your dog chew relentlessly? Opt for metal. Is your dog a nervous traveler? Go with a more portable plastic option.

### Measure for a Correct Fit
To avoid any regrets, ensure to measure your dog for an accurate crate size. A good rule of thumb is to provide your dog enough space to stand up, lie down, and turn around comfortably:

1. Measure your dog's length from the nose to the base of their tail.
2. Measure their height from the ground to the top of their head.
3. Add a few inches to each measurement for comfort.
